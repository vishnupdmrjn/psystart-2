Welcome to Identity Exploration in Emerging Adults!

This course explores identity development during emerging adulthood, focusing on how social, cultural, and emotional factors shape self-concept. Students will engage with key theories like Social Identity Theory and Ecological Systems Theory, gaining insight into how group membership and environmental contexts influence identity. Emphasizing a holistic approach, the course examines how experiences impact self-concept and relationships.
