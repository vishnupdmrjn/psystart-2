Social identity and cultural influences on identity exploration

Interactive Activity

Discussion Board Assignment

Reflect on how your cultural background has influenced your identity or provide an example from literature or media where cultural influences shape a character’s identity

Provide a 150-200 word writeup; The content would be your reflection on how your cultural background has influenced your identity or provide an example from literature or media where cultural influences shape a character’s identity. In your response, consider the following

  • 1. How does your cultural background shape your values, beliefs, and sense of self?
  • 2. In what ways have cultural traditions, family values, or societal expectations influenced your identity development?
  • 3. How do the concepts of social identity, as explained by theories like Social Identity Theory (SIT) or Bronfenbrenner's Ecological Systems Theory, apply to your personal experience or to a character you choose from literature or media?
  • 4. How has your identity evolved over time in response to cultural shifts or life transitions?

If you choose to reflect on a literary or media example, consider:

  • 1. What specific cultural elements shape the character’s identity?
  • 2. How does the character navigate in-group and out-group dynamics, and how does this influence their personal growth?
  • 3. In what ways do cultural expectations create conflict or support for the character’s development?

Assessment (and keywords for answers) - (2x5 = 10 marks)

  • 1. How does Social Identity Theory (SIT) explain the way individuals perceive in-groups and out-groups in emerging adulthood?

    • a. In-group image enhancement
    • b. Negative out-group characteristics
    • c. Identity in sports, politics, social class
    • d. Emerging adulthood

  • 2. What role does Bronfenbrenner's Ecological Systems Theory play in shaping an individual's social identity?

    • a. Social identity formation
    • b. 5 systems: Microsystem, mesosystem, exosystem, macrosystem, chronosystem
    • c. Group memberships
    • d. Contextual impact

  • 3. What are the three key cognitive processes in the formation of social identity according to Tajfel’s Social Identity Theory?

    • a. Categorization
    • b. Identification
    • c. Comparison
    • d. In-group vs. out-group

  • 4. How does the chronosystem in Bronfenbrenner’s Ecological Systems Theory influence social identity?

    • a. Life transitions
    • b. Historical events
    • c. Cultural shifts
    • d. Social identity over time

  • 5. What is the impact of cultural socialization on the development of social identity?>

    • a. Cultural values
    • b. Internalization
    • c. Group membership
    • d. Self-perception
